Bike Signs iOS app

This iOS app displays direction signs when you need to make turn while you are riding a bike. You need an arm band in order to use this app. Launch the app, shake your arm when you need a sing. Shake it again to turn off the sign. Front camera will make a video record of your rear scene when the sign is on. You can check all the records later on your photo album. Ride safe!
